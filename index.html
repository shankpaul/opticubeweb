What is Opticube?

Opticube is desktop application for your opticalshops. The application helps you to manage your customers, Products, Sales and stock in easy and efficient ways. The diffrent modules availbale in our software are Billing, Customers management, Stock & Sales management, Barcode genarations, Barcode reading, Reports, Exporting all report to excel and other formats.
